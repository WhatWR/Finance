Chapter 5 — Bond Valuation & Interest Rates
การคำนวณราคาพันธบัตร (Bond Price), อัตราผลตอบแทน (YTM), และแนวคิดพื้นฐาน

Key Formulas

Bond Price = Σ [C/(1+r)ᵗ] + FV/(1+r)ⁿ
Semi-annual: r = YTM/2, n = years×2
Quarterly: r = YTM/4, n = years×4
PMT = FV × (coupon rate / periods/yr)

= 10.625 × [1−(1.01)⁻¹²]/0.01 + 1000/(1.01)¹²

Chapter 7 — Stock Valuation & EMH
Dividend Discount Model (DDM), P/E Model, Growth Rate, Market Efficiency

Key Formulas

Gordon Model: P₀ = D₁/(Re − g)
D₁ = D₀ × (1+g)
g = ROE × Retention Ratio
Retention Ratio = 1 − Payout Ratio
P₀ = EPS × P/E

WACC = Wd×Rd(1−T) + Wp×Rp + We×Re
Re (CAPM) = Rf + β×MRP
Re (DDM) = D₁/P₀ + g

g = ROE × (1 − Payout)
